# Overview

Prontolind is a professional tattoo and piercing aftercare brand manufactured by Prontomed GmbH, a German medical and cosmetic products company founded in 1998. The Prontolind product line launched in 2007 and is widely used in European piercing and tattoo studios.

For PiercingConnect, Prontolind is a high-priority European professional aftercare brand with medical-device positioning.

# Primary Product Characteristics (Prontolind Spray 75ml)

Documented characteristics include:

* Antimicrobial cleaning via polyhexanide-betaine complex
* Suitable for all piercing types including mucous membrane areas
* Free of alcohol, phenols, and fragrances (brand claim)
* Penetrates piercing canal via spray application
* EAN: 4260177230464
* Available in pharmacies (brand claim, European market)

**Editorial note:** This is not a sterile saline product. It is an antimicrobial aftercare solution based on polyhexanide technology.
