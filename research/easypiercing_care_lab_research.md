# Overview

EasyPiercing is a professional piercing aftercare brand developed and distributed by Medical Body Art (MBA), now marketed under the OTZI by Easypiercing brand umbrella. The product line provides studio-oriented piercing hygiene and aftercare solutions including saline spray, cleansing gel, antibacterial solution, and complete care kits.

For PiercingConnect, EasyPiercing Care Lab is relevant as a European professional B2B aftercare system used in piercing studios.

# Primary Product Characteristics (Saline Solution 50ml)

Documented ingredients (official product page):

* Aqua (Water)
* Sodium Chloride

Documented for aerosol packaging (kit/duopack pages):

* Nitrogen (propellant in pressurized container)

Documented use:

* Daily rinsing and hydration of piercings
* Micro-particle spray diffuser
* PAO: 1 month after opening

**Editorial note:** Sterility status not explicitly confirmed on official product page. This is a saline-based rinse, not necessarily equivalent to sterile wound wash products.
