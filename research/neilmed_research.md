# Overview

NeilMed Pharmaceuticals was founded in 2000 by Dr. Ketan C. Mehta and Nina Mehta. The company is widely known for saline irrigation products and later expanded into dedicated piercing aftercare products.

For PiercingConnect, NeilMed is considered a high-priority brand because it is frequently recommended within the professional piercing community and has dedicated products specifically designed for piercing aftercare.

# Community Reputation

Across piercing communities, NeilMed is commonly cited as a preferred sterile saline spray, largely because of its fine mist applicator and simple formulation. Community discussions also emphasize that other sterile 0.9% saline sprays with only water and sodium chloride can be appropriate alternatives when NeilMed is unavailable. Community opinions are informative but are not treated as primary evidence.
