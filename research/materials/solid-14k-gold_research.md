# Overview

Solid 14 karat gold may be appropriate for initial piercings when it is nickel- and cadmium-free and alloyed for biocompatibility per APP initial jewelry guidance. This record refers to solid gold body jewelry, not plated or gold-filled products. Alloy composition and vendor verification matter because not all gold jewelry meets APP criteria.

# Evidence vs Marketing

* Material type in structured data: `precious-metal`
* APP alignment in structured data: `acceptable-with-caution`
* Suitable for fresh piercings (editorial flag): `caution`
* Suitable for healed piercings (editorial flag): `true`
* Allergy risk (editorial flag): `moderate`

Marketing labels such as "hypoallergenic," "surgical," or "implant quality" without ASTM/ISO documentation or piercer verification must not be treated as primary evidence.
