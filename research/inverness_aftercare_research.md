# Overview

Inverness Corporation manufactures the Inverness Ear Piercing System and a companion ear care aftercare solution. The brand is distributed globally through retail, jewelry, salon, pharmacy, and medical settings in 70+ countries. Inverness is primarily an ear-piercing systems company rather than a general body piercing aftercare brand.

For PiercingConnect, Inverness Aftercare is relevant as a retail ear-piercing channel product commonly bundled with piercing services.

# Primary Product Characteristics (Ear Care Solution)

Documented on official ingredients page:

* Purified water
* Benzalkonium chloride (antiseptic)

Documented product claims:

* Formulated for newly pierced ears
* Dermatologist-tested and non-irritating (official piercing system page)
* Vegan and cruelty-free (official piercing system page)
* Alternative to isopropyl alcohol and hydrogen peroxide for ear piercing aftercare (brand positioning)

**Editorial note:** This is an antiseptic solution, not sterile saline. Primarily positioned for ear piercings. Benzalkonium chloride–based products differ from APP-recommended sterile saline aftercare.
