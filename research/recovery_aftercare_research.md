# Overview

Recovery Aftercare is a tattoo and piercing aftercare brand offering saline washes, soaps, oils, and related recovery products. The brand was founded in 2011 with a focus on all-natural, vegan-friendly, and environmentally conscious aftercare. Recovery is widely distributed through professional supply channels including Painful Pleasures.

For PiercingConnect, Recovery Aftercare is a high-priority US professional-channel sterile saline brand.
