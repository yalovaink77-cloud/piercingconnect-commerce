# Overview

Base Laboratories (marketed as Base Labs) is a Los Angeles, California–based company specializing in over-the-counter health, wellness, and cosmetic products. The brand offers a dedicated piercing aftercare product line including saline sprays, hypochlorous acid sprays, bump care oils, and related skin solutions.

For PiercingConnect, Base Laboratories is relevant as a widely distributed US piercing aftercare brand with both simple saline and enhanced-formula products.

# Primary Product Characteristics (Piercing Aftercare Spray)

Manufacturer-described characteristics include:

* Marketed as sterile saline mist
* USP Grade Water
* USP Sodium Chloride (pharmaceutical saline)
* Additional ingredients: tea tree oil, allantoin, aloe vera, rosemary leaf oil, vitamin B5 (panthenol)
* Fine mist, touch-free application
* Suitable for ears, nose, cartilage, nipple, and body piercings

**Editorial note:** This product is not a simple two-ingredient sterile saline spray. The added botanical and essential oil ingredients require careful editorial positioning relative to APP-style sterile saline guidance.
