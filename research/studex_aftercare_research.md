# Overview

STUDEX is a global ear piercing systems manufacturer that also produces a dedicated aftercare product line. The brand is widely associated with mall, retail, and professional ear piercing contexts. Studex aftercare products include lotions, gels, and the Advanced 2-in-1 Piercing Aftercare & Cleanser.

For PiercingConnect, Studex Aftercare is relevant as a retail-channel aftercare brand with strong ear-piercing market presence.

# Primary Product Characteristics (Advanced 2-in-1)

Documented on official product page:

* Hypochlorous formula (HypoBiome Technology)
* Ingredients: Water, sodium hypochlorite, phosphoric acid
* Alcohol free, benzalkonium chloride free (explicit product claim)
* Hypoallergenic, dermatologist and pediatrician tested
* Vegan and cruelty-free (product claim)
* Manufactured in accordance with cGMP and international standards (product claim)
* SKU: R901-A

Documented use:

* Spray at least twice daily on front and back of piercing
* 3 weeks for ear lobe; up to 8 weeks for helix/cartilage

**Editorial note:** This is a hypochlorous-based cleanser, not sterile saline. Healing timeline claims on packaging require cautious editorial framing.
