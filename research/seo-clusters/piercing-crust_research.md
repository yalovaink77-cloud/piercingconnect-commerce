# Safety Notes

* Crusting may be normal during healing; do not diagnose infection from crust alone
* Spray saline and gently rinse; avoid picking or forcibly removing crust
* Thick colored discharge with other concern signs requires professional evaluation
* Do not recommend picking, alcohol, or peroxide to remove crust
