# Knowledge Taxonomy

Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Project: PiercingConnect Commerce Blueprint

---

## Purpose

This document defines the core taxonomy of the PiercingConnect Knowledge Graph.

The taxonomy exists to make piercing knowledge structured, searchable, reusable, and machine-readable.

It will support:

* books
* structured data
* SEO clusters
* affiliate strategy
* product comparisons
* problem-solving content
* PC Media Engine publishing workflows

---

## Core Principle

Every piece of knowledge should belong to a clear category.

Every category should support at least one of these goals:

* safety
* education
* SEO
* affiliate revenue
* publishing automation
* professional reference

## Taxonomy Rules

### Rule 1 — No Orphan Knowledge

Every knowledge item must connect to at least one larger category.

---

### Rule 2 — Safety Before Commercial Classification

If an entity affects health or safety, it must be classified medically before commercially.

---

### Rule 3 — Problems Are Not Diagnoses

Problem content must educate users without pretending to diagnose medical conditions.

---

### Rule 4 — Products Are Not Solutions by Default

A product may support aftercare, but content must not imply that a product can cure medical conditions.

---

### Rule 5 — Affiliate Data Must Be Separate

Affiliate value must be stored separately from safety or quality evaluation.

---

### Rule 6 — Countries Matter

Availability, regulation, shipping, and affiliate programs may differ by country.

Country data should not be ignored.

---

### Rule 7 — Templates Are Outputs

Templates should generate content from knowledge.

Templates should not become the source of knowledge.

---
