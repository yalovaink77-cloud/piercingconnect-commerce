# Roadmap

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Project: PiercingConnect Commercial Knowledge Core

---

## Current Phase: v1.0 Stabilization (Complete)

Repository structure, taxonomy, templates, and Book 1 knowledge chapters are in place for PC Media Engine integration.

---

## Phase 2 — Revenue Engine (Next)

- PCME ingestion pipeline from `data/` and `templates/`
- Validation scripts for cross-link integrity
- Export packages for WordPress, email, and social channels
- Affiliate link registry wired to `data/affiliate-programs/`
- Review workflow to promote entities from `draft` to `active`

---

## Phase 3 — Publishing Automation

- First automated publish set from MVP SEO clusters
- Comparison and buying guide generation (NeilMed vs H2Ocean, saline guides)
- Problem-guide publishing for high-priority clusters
- Channel-specific output QA checklist

---

## Phase 4 — Knowledge Expansion

- Additional brands and products beyond MVP set
- Book 2 Jewelry Bible population
- Country-specific availability records
- Multilingual content strategy (no translation until English core is stable)

---

## Phase 5 — Premium and Studio Products

- PDF guide exports from Book 1
- Studio-facing professional resources
- Premium education packaging

---

## Non-Goals (v1)

- Inventing affiliate commission rates in structured data
- Regional legal claims without verified sources
- Blog-style articles stored as source of truth
- Renumbering Book 1 chapters (legacy slots preserved as navigation)

---

## Review Triggers

Revisit roadmap when:

- APP or major aftercare guidance updates
- Affiliate program structure changes
- PCME integration requirements change
- New product categories enter the catalog
